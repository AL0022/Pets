Why fish are supposedly so great.

  • Doesn't need to go to the vet,
  • Replaceable,
  • Easy to care for.
  • Not picky eaters will eat everything that it can fit in it's mouth until it dies.

What fish like to eat

  • Algae eaters like to eat algae. Hence the name.
  • When tank shining clean just drop him a tiny ball of compact algae. NOT TOO MUCH OR HE WILL DIE FROM EATING TOO MUCH IM NOT JOKING FISH DON'T KNOW WHEN TO STOP EATING.
  • Fish flakes will do for tropical fish. Read above, They aren't picky eaters.
